STUDENT OUTCOMES

I can/I have

Student outcomes refer to the desired learning objectives or standards that schools and teachers aim for students to achieve, as well as the educational, societal, and life effects resulting from students being educated. They are often expressed as measurable statements that specify what learners will know or be able to do after completing a course. Overall, student outcomes are critical for assessing the effectiveness of educational systems and ensuring that all students reach their full potential.
